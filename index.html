About the Artist

Nasim Hoor / SUN HOOR

Nasim Hoor is a London-based multidisciplinary visual artist whose practice spans painting, Persian calligraphy, sculpture, printmaking, drawing and mixed-media experimentation. Her visual language begins with the discipline of Persian calligraphy, where she learned rhythm, gesture, balance and silence through the movement of the line.

Her training in graphic design and architecture, together with more than fourteen years of university-level art and visual communication teaching, has shaped a practice that combines structure with intuition. In her work, calligraphic traces are often transformed into energetic marks, abstract compositions, textured surfaces and sculptural forms.

For SUN HOOR, colour is emotional and architectural: bold, layered and intentional. Her works carry memories of Persian visual culture while moving toward a contemporary language of movement, transformation and inner expression.
